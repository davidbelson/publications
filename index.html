David Belson - Publications

Over the last 25 years, I have written a significant amount of Internet and Web-related content. I enjoy making highly technical topics more accessible to a broader audience, as well as telling stories with data.

  1. LAST MONTH IN INTERNET INTELLIGENCE - Monthly blog post series that reviewed significant Internet disruptions identified each month through the Oracle Internet Intelligence Map. Required research to identify root causes for identifed outages. Published July 2018 - March 2019. [VIEW ARCHIVE]
  2. ORACLE INTERNET INTELLIGENCE BLOG POSTS - Posts covering a variety of topics leveraging various Oracle Internet Intelligence data sets. Published September 2017 - June 2018. [Federal IPv6 Adoption | Tracking CDN Usage | SeaMeWe-3 Cable Break | ACE Submarine Cable Cut | Brazilian Power Failure | South Korea Internet Connectivity | Migration of Political Internet Shutdowns | Internet Availability | Internet Impacts of Hurricanes | Breaking the Internet]
  3. WALL STREET JOURNAL SPONSORED CONTENT - Year-end articles summarizing Internet disruptions identified through Oracle Internet Intelligence tools and data. Published February 2019, December 2017. [Why the Internet Goes Dark: Politics, Cable Cuts…and Exam Day | 2017 in Review: Year of Global Internet Disruptions]
  4. FINDING YOURSELF: THE CHALLENGES OF ACCURATE GEOLOCATION - Multi-format program highlighting the challenges of accurately geolocating IP addresses, issues with existing solutions, the importance of using accurate IP address geolocation information, and the impacts of getting it wrong. Published January 2018. [BLOG POST, EBOOK, WEBINAR]
  5. AKAMAI STATE OF THE INTERNET/CONNECTIVITY REPORT - A quarterly report series highlighting Internet connection speeds (fixed & mobile) around the world, IPv4 exhaustion and IPv6 adoption trends, browser usage trends, and Internet outages, based on data exhaust from Akamai's services and the Akamai Intelligent platform. Grew to generate hundreds of pieces of earned media coverage around the world each quarter. Published Q1 2008 - Q1 2017. [VIEW ARCHIVE]
  6. AKAMAI STATE OF THE INTERNET BLOG POSTS - Posts covering a variety of State of the Internet-related topics, including historical trending, broadband connectivity, Internet outages, and changes to covered metrics. Published 2011 - 2017. [VIEW ARCHIVE]
  7. HOW WILL THE INTERNET SCALE? - An Akamai white paper that examined the benefits of a distributed architectural model for supporting hundreds of Terabits/second (Tbps) of Web traffic. Published 2008. [READ]
  8. WEB DEVELOPER MAGAZINE - A series of articles published in Mecklermedia's short-lived Web Developer magazine. Written in conjunction with the Webmasters' Guild. Published 1996-7. [Who's Using Java? Survey Results | A Brief History of Webmastery | You Want It When?]
  9. DAVE RAGGETT - THE WORKING DRAFT - A professional biography of Dr. Dave Raggett, a key contributor to early World Wide Web standards development. Published Summer 1996 in the World Wide Web Journal from O'Reilly Media. [READ]
  10. THE NETWORK NATION REVISITED - Bachelors Thesis. A critical review of The Network Nation to assess the impact that Computer Mediated Communication has had since the book's publication in 1978. Published May 1994. [READ]
